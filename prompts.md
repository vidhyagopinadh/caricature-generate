# Caricature Generation Prompts

This document compiles the image generation prompts used to generate the high-quality 1024x1024 professional 2D vector caricatures for the employees.

---

## 1. Sarah Chen (Senior Staff Engineer, Product)
- **Reference Image**: `sarah_chen_original.png`
- **Generated File**: `caricature_highres_1.png` (mapped to `caricature_1.png` and `sarah_chen.png`)
- **Prompt**:
  > A high-quality 1024x1024 professional 2D vector illustration avatar of Sarah Chen. Maintain the exact facial features, hair style, and likeness of the reference photo of Sarah Chen but translated into a clean cartoon character. The character is looking directly at the camera with a warm, confident smile, wearing professional attire (dark blue blazer and grey top), a company lanyard, and holding a sleek modern laptop. Style: Modern corporate cartoon caricature, clean bold outlines, flat cell shading with subtle gradients, vibrant and cheerful color palette, high-quality digital art sticker style. Background: Bright, colorful, abstract geometric pop-art background featuring clean shapes (triangles, circles, sunbursts, and squiggles) on a crisp white backdrop. Exclusions: NO realistic photo elements, NO dark filters, NO complex background environments, NO messy lines.
